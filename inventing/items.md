# Korinn's Construction Compendium

This compendium gives a list of magical item recipes.

## Weavestone

_See the [Magic Items](../magic-items/misc.md#weavestone) section for the stat block._

A common tool and component used by artificers. A weavestone is created by focussing a large amount of magical spell
power into a pristine gem. The quality of the weavestone is determined by the gem used.

- a gem
- spend three spell slots of the minimum level shown in the table below (others can lend their magic to aid you)

| quality   | stone     | price   | spell level |
|-----------|-----------|---------|-------------|
| common    | Azurite   | 10 gp   | 3           |
| uncommon  | Moonstone | 50 gp   | 3           |
| rare      | Jade      | 100 gp  | 4           |
| very rare | Topaz     | 500 gp  | 4           |
| legendary | Sapphire  | 1000 gp | 5           |
| artifact  | Diamond   | 5000 gp | 6           |

## Sarcen

_See the [Netherese](../magic-items/netherese.md#sarcen) section for the stat block._
