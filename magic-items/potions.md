# Potions

## Draught of the Berserker

After you consume this potion, for 10 minutes, you gain a +2 to all melee weapon attack rolls and all damage to you is reduced by 1d4 (rolled each time).

After these effects has worn of, you gain a level of exhaustion and cannot benefit from the effects of this potion for 60 days.

However, you still gain the level of exhaustion when drinking the potion again within this period, but the original 60 day period is not extended when you do so.
The draught cannot increase your level of exhaustion past level 4.
