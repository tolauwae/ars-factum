# Miscellaneous Magic Items

## Coin of Home

_Wondrous Item, Uncommon, Attunement_

Once per day, as an action you can flip this coin and you instantly teleport home.

While attuned to the coin, you can mark any place you have slept in for at least seven days the last 30 days as your
home. This removes the previous mark. The mark stays as long as you remain attuned to the coin.

## Lens of Truesight

_Wondrous Item, Legendary_

This glass lens has a diameter of 1 foot, but is extremely heavy, weighing approximately 500 pounds. (requiring a
strength of 17 to lift)

Anything seen through the lens is as if it was seen with Truesight.

> A creature with Truesight can (1) see in normal and magical Darkness, see Invisible Creatures and Objects,
> automatically detect visual illusions and succeed on Saving Throws against them, and perceive the original form of a
> Shapechanger or a creature that is transformed by magic. (2) Furthermore, the creature can see into the Ethereal
> Plane.

However, only one side gives you benefit (1), while the other side can exclusively let you see into the Ethereal Plane.

The magic the lens holds, is so strong that it makes the glass foggy over time. After 30 days, the lens is no longer
see-through. To restore it, a single vial of *Oil of Etherealness* has to be applied to the surface of the lens.

## Orb of Teleportation

_Wondrous item (orb), very rare_

This magical orb can teleport a group of creatures to any location. A spellcaster can telepathically engrave a location
on the orb's surface by holding the orb for 10 minutes in deep focus. The creature must know the location and be
familiar with it in great detail. Once engraved the orb is forever tied to this location.

As an action, a creature may peer into the orb to get a glimpse of the location it is tied to. You mentally receive
visual information from the location as if you where there, with normal vision and darkvision out to 30 feet.

As an action you can shatter this orb, releasing a magical sphere with a radius of 20 feet. Any character within this
sphere is teleported to the chosen location. The orb is irretrievably lost.

When you find this orb there is a chance it has already been engraved with a location.

| d100     | location                                  |
|----------|-------------------------------------------|
| 1 - 20   | a random location you have visited before |
| 21 - 40  | a random location on an elemental plane   |
| 41 - 60  | a random location on the material plane   |
| 61 - 80  | the place where you found the orb         |
| 81 - 100 | no location                               |

## Orb of Remembering

_Wondrous item (orb), uncommon (requires attunement)_

This orbs works as a mental library. You can mentally write down anything in it. Reading and writing information takes
as much time as with pen and paper.

When you lose attunement to the orb, it loses everything that is written down in it.

## Traveler's Flask

_Wondrous item, common_

This double-sided iron flask adjusts the temperature of liquids poured from it. Any liquid poured from the blue-capped
side becomes refreshingly cool. Any liquid poured from the red-capped side becomes soothingly warm.

## Weavestone

_Wondrous item, rarity varies_

This magical gem is a common tool and component used by artificers. A weave gem is created by focussing a large amount
of magical spell power into a pristine gem. The quality of the weavestone is determined by the gem used.

A spellcaster can recover some of the magic within the weavestone. You may spend a bonus action while holding the gem to
recover a level three spell slot immediately by spending a level one spell slot. Afterward, the gem turns into dust
losing all magical power.

A sorcerer can take the stone and siphon its magical power, the stone returns to a normal non-magical gem and the
sorcerer regains 1 sorcery point.

An artificer can take the stone and as an action pulverize it into a cloud of magical dust. The dust spreads into a
30ft. sphere, the sphere reveals magical effects, magical items, and even the imprint of recent spells cast within the
last hour, by tracing them into a faint blue glow. For every rarity level above common the gem spreads out an additional
20ft. and can reveal magic from an additional hour ago. The artificer knows the school of the magic if applicable and
can make an Arcana Check to identify the specific spell or item immediately. The dust and its effects disappear after 10
minutes.

An artificer can take the stone and use its magic to turn a mundane item into a magical one. You may use your infuse
item feature ignoring the normal item limit. The gem is fused into the item by performing this action and lost forever.
The effects of the infuse item only last for 1 hour. The effects last for an additional two hours for every rarity level
above common.

When you throw the stone it explodes into a thousand magical sparks. The gem is destroyed but deals 3d8 + 3 force damage
to whatever object or creature it hit.

If the stone is very rare or higher, a spell caster may also absorb the power of the gem to empower themselves, the
stone returns to a normal non-magical gem and the spellcaster gains a +5 bonus to their Spell Save DC.

If the stone is of artifact level you can use a bonus action while holding it to crush it and tear open a brief stitch
in the Weave at a point you can see within 60 feet. The stone is destroyed, and you choose one of the following effects:

- You cast dispel magic at 9th level.
- You choose a target within range, for 1 hour whenever the target attempts to cast a spell they must succeed on a DC
  18 spellcasting ability check; on a failure, the spell takes no effect but any spell slots or components are spent as
  normal.
