# C-Y-R IV

_Medium construct, true neutral_
![](/images/stat-block-header-bar.svg)

**Armor Class** 16 + 5 (natural armor + shield)

**Hit Points** 165 (10d20+60)

**Speed** 50 ft., swim 40 ft., climb 40 ft.

![](/images/stat-block-header-bar.svg)

**STR**  21 (+5)  **DEX** 25 (+7)  **CON** 27 (+8)  **INT** 16 (+3)  **WIS** 13 (+1)  **CHA** 15 (+2)

![](/images/stat-block-header-bar.svg)

**Saving Throws** Dex +13, Con +12, Int +9

**Damage Immunities** Poison

**Condition Immunities** Diseased

**Senses** Darkvision 120 ft. (magical), passive perception 19

**Languages** all languages

**Challenge** 18 (20000 XP)

**Proficiency Bonus** +6

![](/images/stat-block-header-bar.svg)

_**Martial Arts Savant**._ C-Y-R IV has proficiency with unarmed strikes and can use shields with melee weapons with the throw property.

_**Agile Build**._ C-Y-R IV has been designed to be agile and fast, it ignores difficult terrain and as a bonus action it can take the disengage or dash actions.

_**Legendary Attuner.**_ C-Y-R IV can attune to up to eight magic items, and it can attune to any magic item ignoring all requirements.

_**Special Equipment.**_ C-Y-R IV is attuned to his right hand, his shield.

_**Constructed Nature.**_ As a magical construct, C-Y-R IV has the following benefits:
 - It doesn't need to eat, drink, or breathe.
 - It is immune to disease.
 - It doesn't need to sleep, and magic can't put it to sleep.

_**Magic Resistance.**_ C-Y-R IV has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**ACTIONS**
![](/images/stat-block-header-bar.svg)

_**Multiattack.**_ C-Y-R IV makes two *Unarmed Strikes* and one *Throw Shield* or *Shield Bash* attack. As part of this action, it can use its *Return Weapon* ability.

_**Unarmed strike.**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +13 to hit, reach 10ft., one target. _Hit:_ 17 (2d6 + 10) magical bludgeoning damage and 7 (2d6) thunder damage.

_**Throw Shield.**_ _Ranged Weapon Attack:_ +13 to hit, reach 80ft., one target. _Hit:_ 13 (1d6 + 10) magical slashing damage. The shield can bounce up to two times to an additional target in 15ft of the previous target. The shield can only hit a target once.

_**Shield Bash.**_ Melee Weapon Attack:_ +15 to hit, reach 10ft., one target. _Hit:_ 13 (1d6 + 10) magical bludgeoning damage. The target is pushed back in a straight line of 20ft.

_**Haymaker (recharge 3-6).**_ _Melee Weapon Attack:_ +13 to hit, reach 10ft., one target. _Hit:_ 34 (4d10 + 12) magical bludgeoning damage plus 14 (4d6) thunder damage. The target must succeed on a DC 20 Constitution saving throw or be **stunned** until the end of C‑Y‑R IV’s next turn. On a successful save, the target takes half the damage and suffers no additional effects. 

_**Meteoric Strike (recharge 5-6).**_  C-Y-R IV uses his fists to create a large earthquake in a 90 foot cone. Each creature in the area must make a DC 19 Dexterity Saving Throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 49 (9d10) magical bludgeoning damage, and is knocked prone. On a successful save, the creature takes half as much damage with no additional effects. The terrain in the area becomes difficult terrain for 1 hour.

_**Ultimate Defence (Recharge 5-6).**_ C-Y-R IV sheaths all his weapons and uses its arms to shield itself from attack while its mechanical body repairs itself. Until the start of its next turn, C-Y-R IV has +5 AC and recovers 28 (3d10 + 12) hit points at the start of each creature's turn. During this time, it cannot use reactions and its speed is 0.

_**Automatic Self-destruct.**_ C-Y-R IV self-destructs upon its death. All items it is holding and those that are kept in its *scabbard (bag of holding)* are destroyed, except magical artifacts. The blast scorches an area of 20 feet around C-Y-R IX. Within the area, the explosion ignites flammable objects, and every creature must make a Dexterity Saving Throw. On a fail, a target takes 74 (8d12 + 22) fire damage.

**BONUS ACTION**
![](/images/stat-block-header-bar.svg)

_**Return Weapon.**_ As a bonus action, C-Y-R IV can use its right hand to return any metal weapon to him.

**REACTIONS**
![](/images/stat-block-header-bar.svg)

_**Attack of Opportunity.**_ Whenever a creature moves out or into the range of C-Y-R IV's melee attacks, it can make a single attack with his current weapon.

_**Evasive Action.**_ Whenever a creature hits C-Y-R IV with a melee attacks, it can use its reaction to half the damage.

**LEGENDARY ACTIONS**
![](/images/stat-block-header-bar.svg)

C-Y-R IV can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from the options below. Only one legendary action option can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. C-Y-R IV regains spent legendary actions at the start of its turn.

_**Shockwave Return.**_ _Range Attack:_ +13 to hit, reach 90ft., radius 20ft. If his shield is not within 10ft of C-Y-R IV, he stretches out his shield arm as his shield flies back to him. The shield slams into C-Y-R IV arm with a thunderous clap. Each creature in a 20 ft radius must make a DC 20 Dexterity Saving Throw. On a failure, the creature is push back 10 ft and takes 21 (6d6) thunder damage.

_**Strategic Positioning.**_ C-Y-R IV can move up to half its speed without provoking opportunity attacks.

_**Ready (Costs 2 actions).**_ C-Y-R IV readies itself to retaliate against incoming attacks. It recovers a reaction if possible. Until the start of its next turn, C-Y-R IV has advantage on attacks.

_**Disarm (Costs 3 actions).**_ C-Y-R IV can attempt to take an object a creature within 10 feet is holding. The creature must win a Strength Contest, or lose the object. Any object retrieved in this way is placed inside the scabbard of C-Y-R IV.
